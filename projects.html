Projects:

Hextech Hammer

The first and hopefully last weapon built with hextech. This hammer was designed to allow a single person to take on a large group of foes. Equipped with massive power, shields, and the capability to shoot projectiles energy attacks. A skilled person could take the place of many enforcers.


Hex Claw

A mechanical arm equipped with a powerful ray of light. Designed to greatly increase the productivity of the local artificers. No longer needing to ship out or wait for parts to be delivered, this device will help increase our rate of creation.


Atlas Gauntlets

A powerful set of gauntlets that are designed to help miners and fishermen with their daily labor. Reducing their daily fatigue, increasing production and their quality of life at the same time. These gauntlets are strong enough to lift and crush a boulder single handedly.
