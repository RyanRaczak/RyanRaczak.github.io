Jayce Talis

The Man of Progress!

Jayce Talis is a brilliant inventor who has pledged his life to the defense of Piltover and its unyielding pursuit of progress. With his transforming hextech hammer in hand, Jayce uses his strength, courage, and considerable intelligence to protect his hometown. While revered throughout the city as a hero, he hasn't taken well to the attention it brings. Still, Jayce's heart is in the right place, and even those who envy his natural skills are grateful for his protection in the City of Progress.


Jayce believes in progress and many unheard voices hinder that progress. If you feel like you have a great idea that could change the future, please email Councillor Talis
